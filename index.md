# ucsb-ds.github.io

This site documents resources for students and faculty studying
Data Science at UCSB.

There are various units at UCSB that offer Data Science instruction, including, but not limited to:

* The [Department of Statstics and Applied Probability](https://www.pstat.ucsb.edu/) which offers *undergraduate degrees* and advanced course work in Data Science.
* The [Department of Computer Science](https://cs.ucsb.edu) which offers two *courses for non-majors* in Data Science (CMPSC 5A and CMPSC 5B)
* Various courses across L&S, Bren, and Gevirtz that teach data science concepts in the context of their own academic disciplines.
